Bebecup is an on-device baby-photo curation app. From the many near-identical shots piling up in your gallery, it quietly trims the blurry and closed-eye ones, finds this week's best moments, and turns them into a short shortlist for parents to confirm. The final choice always belongs to you — and your picks flow straight into a family photo tournament.

Key features
- AI picks: recent photos are analyzed on-device to suggest the best candidates
- Blur, face, eye, and expression analysis with simple, friendly reasons
- Near-duplicate (burst) photos are reduced to one representative shot
- Parent approval: keep, exclude, or re-add any AI suggestion
- Family tournament: crown the single best photo from your shortlist
- Organize winners for printing and hand off to an external print site

Privacy
- All photo analysis runs on-device; photos are never uploaded to a server.
- No ads or tracking SDKs.
- Excluded photos are never deleted, and analysis data can be wiped anytime in Settings.

Many apps edit photos. Bebecup focuses on helping parents quickly choose the single best baby photo from hundreds of similar memories.
